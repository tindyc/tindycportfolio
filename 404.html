About Me

Hello! My name is Tindy. I am based in London with my two chinchillas- Chubby and Klaus. I write, design and am very much into coding nowadays. I have always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns — from studying politics, law and philosophy, to working in the business industry and software development — I've never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems.

As a Web Developer, I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python languages, using Django, Flask and React frameworks. I am extra passionate about creating a positive user experience. Each site I develop is designed to be search engine friendly, using the latest development principles and techniques as they develop. I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for creating things and my mission-driven work ethic to work with people with brilliant ideas.
